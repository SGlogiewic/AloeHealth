Aside from emergency surgeries that need to be performed on short notice, Aloe Health's doctors and surgeons offer several non-emergency surgeries that can provide patients with a better quality of life.

Spinal Fusion

Spinal fusion is a surgery to prevent spinal pain in patients. The procedure fuses two or more vertebrae in the spine to any movement between them that may be causing pain.

Estimated cost: $39,000 - $50,000

Hip Replacement Surgery

Hip replacement surgery replaces the hip joint with a prosthetic implant. Price varies widely for hip replacements, as either the enite hip joint can be replaced, or only part of the hip joint can be replaced.

Estimated cost: $20,000 - $50,000

Cataract Surgery (A.K.A. Lens Replacement Surgery)

Cataract surgery is a surgery where the natural lens of the eyeball is removed, typically due to the development of cataract on the lens of the eyeball. The lens is then replaced with an articial eyeball lens.

Estimated cost: $3,500 - $6,000

While mental illnesses and mental pain are not physical, imbalances in the brain can cause a multitude of issues throughout the body which is why it is important that action is taken to remedy, or at the very least control, mental issues before they manifest into physical illnesses.

Therapy costs vary significantly depending on the therapist and the amount of appointments the patient requests. Please consult with a therapist about pricing before scheduling an appointment.

Behavioral Therapy

Behavioral therapy is a an umbrella term for similar therapies that seeks to identify and help change destructive or problematic behaviors in the patient.

This therapy is typically used in patients with: depression, anxiety, panic disorders, anger mangement issues

Cognitive Therapy

Cognitive therapy is a type of therapy that is emotionally-driven and goal-oriented.

This method of therapy is most often used to treat patients dealing with extreme grief, loss, or stress. Patients with depression or anxiety may also benefit form cognitive therapy.

Family Therapy

Family therapy is a type of psychological counseling (psychotherapy).

Family therapy is most often used to help family members improve communication and resolve conflicts. It is also commonly used to help abused children.
